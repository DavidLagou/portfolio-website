About me

I Am A Front End Developer Based In Vancouver BC

I am currently attending BCIT where I study New Media and Web Development. At BCIT, I focus on designing user interfaces and creating websites that help people grow their businesses. As someone who has a curiosity on human cognitive behaviour, being able to apply those theories not only excites me , but helps me when it comes to constructing potential layouts.


Before BCIT, I studied Interactive Arts and Technology at Simon Fraser University where I also played on the Varsity Football Team. Football, a sport I’ve played for many years has bestowed upon me the gift of leadership, problem solving and most importantly patience.


When I’m not at my computer, I spend most of my time cycling or camping with family and friends.
